Investigation Overview¶

Through this presentaion we will try to answer 2 Questions :
1- who uses Bike Share service more ?
2- who uses Bike Share service longer ?

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset "Ford GoBike System Data" consists of 900,000 rows and 15 columns. Columns include features like : gender, age , user type, trip start time , trip end time, bike ID

Users ages vs the number of times the service is used¶

Over the past decade, bike-share has been one of the great U.S transportation success stories. Many people ,especially young ones, used it as a main transportation method for going and returning from workplace. The following visualisation shows service use frequency during Feb 2019 associated with every age range

Does age effect ?¶

Younger people tend to depend more on bike-share service, so they tend to be subscribers more than customers, in contrast older ones tend to use it when they need it, which make them customers.

Variation in the service usage during the day , and week¶

As i said before, people tend to depend on bike-share service as a main transportaion method. The following graph shows that the peak hours of the service usage is when people go to their work. Then, the number decreases until leaving from work at 5 and 6 p.m. The graph at the right also shows service usages during each day of the week.

The average trip duration for subscribers vs customers¶

Although the ratio of customers is very low compared to subscribers, but when they use the service they tend to use it longer than subscribers with considerable margin, which is a good sign of understading the importance of this service. With some effort these customers can be converted to subscribers !
